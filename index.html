3DIRV

3D Impulse Response Visualization

This innovation tool allows satisfying requirements of the audio industry, noise control and acoustics. 3DIRV is a free and simple software for use whit a low-cost equipment, whit the aims to break the current acoustic measurement standards. Among the services to offer can be named:

  • Customized developments, adaptations or integrations or Consulting
  • Validations to comply with certain regulations
  • Calibrations
  • Measurements and characterizations
  • Third-party product recommendations compatible with the tool
  • Commercialization of recommended equipment
  • Installation of static measuring systems

Pressure Analisis

When the audio files are selected, the program perform all processing and display the pressure level in time domain as we are use to. Also the selected windows are plotted in green points. The direct sound is plotted in red line.

Intensity Analysis

In order to get a better understanding of the measurement set, the intensity level are shown in a second tab. Also the selected windows are plotted in green points. The direct sound is plotted in red line.

Window Analysis

The processing perform a window integration of the intensity levels. The time integration window is selected in the window size input box. Also the selected windows are plotted in green points. The direct sound is plotted in red line.

Filter Response

In order to avoid phase distortion the signal is passed thought a low-pass filter. The normalize response of the filter is showed.

3D Impulse Response Visualization

The seven colors of the spikes that goes from red to blue indicates the time in milliseconds after the direct sound (black) arrives. The lengths of each one of the spikes correspond to the amplitude of this averaged intensity.These amplitudes are normalized based on the direct sound.
